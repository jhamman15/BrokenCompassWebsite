Knowledge is Power!

Knowing what you are getting into is an absolute neccesity when you are venturing onto a trail or camping trip. Many trails can turn dangerous and even deadly depending on the conditions. Putting some time into learning the area or looking at maps could save your life.

Check the Weather!

Weather

Being alert and aware of the weather and planning for the weather is vital. Always bring proper gear and plan for the worst. Check weather reports and look for updated information from the ranger stations. Weather can turn quickly epsecially as you get to higher elevation.

Animals

Knowing what types of animals are around in the area you are exploring is good to know especially when there can be potentially dangerous animals. Check for rules and regulations with bear spray and keep an eye out for poisonous snakes if you are in that type of area.
